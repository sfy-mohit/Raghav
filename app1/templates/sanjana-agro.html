Nestled in the heart of our sprawling landscape lies a sanctuary of poultry excellence – a testament to our unwavering commitment to every feathered inhabitant. At our integrated poultry farm, we embark on a holistic journey that encompasses every stage of growth, nurturing and production. Our dedication is evident from our comprehensive offerings: Boiler Breeder Farming, Hatching Eggs, One Day Old Chicks and the cornerstone of it all, a well-tended Poultry Farm.

Boiler Breeder Farming, the first step in our journey, is a testament to our meticulous approach. We raise mature parent birds with utmost care, prioritizing their health, genetics and overall well-being. These birds stand as the backbone of our operation, forming the foundation of superior egg production.

Since

2017

From these select parent birds, we harvest Hatching Eggs that embody the promise of new beginnings. Carefully chosen for their quality, these eggs hold within them the potential for robust and vibrant life. Our expertise in genetics and incubation ensures that each hatching egg carries the essence of future growth, packed with the potential to transform into strong, healthy chicks.

Our One Day Old Chicks are the embodiment of our dedication to the poultry venture. Born within the first 24 hours, these chicks represent more than just mere products, they embody the promise of a healthy start, primed to thrive under your care. From the moment they hatch, they symbolize growth, vitality and a future ripe with possibilities. From the carefully designed living spaces to the tailored nutrition plans.
